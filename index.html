Welcome Home

For Sale

Tucked into the sought-after Maple Bluff neighborhood, this modern 2016-built home offers 2,400 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space on a generous third-of-an-acre lot. With three bedrooms, three full bathrooms, and a dedicated exercise room that easily converts to a fourth bedroom, there is room for the whole family to spread out.


Soaring high ceilings and an open floor plan fill the main level with natural light, anchored by a stunning kitchen featuring a professional BlueStar range, quartz countertops, a walk-in pantry, and a charming breakfast nook. The finished basement adds even more living and entertaining space, complete with its own fireplace and barn door accents.


Step outside to a large, fully fenced private backyard — perfect for kids, pets, and summer entertaining. Practical upgrades include solar panels that bring your electricity bill to virtually zero, a whole-home security system, smart home features, a mudroom with built-in dog spa, radon mitigation, and a sump pump. The extra-large garage offers two full bays plus a third tandem spot for additional storage or parking.
